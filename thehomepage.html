Why Join Us?

Led by an all-girls team of travel directors Girls Who Travel has offered tours that encourage female solo travelers to experience the world in the most meaningful ways since the year of 2017.

At Girls Who Travel, we pride ourselves in creating a sense of connection to the places you visit and the people you meet by keeping a maximum of 10 women per trip. We also have locally based tour guides who always are enthusiastic to show you the best their cities have to offer.

Our trips take you to settings where trying new ventures is inspired. We also include opportunities for cultural exchange with local communities in the city where you will be visiting. You will have the chance to learn how to cook the most delicious food and enjoy a meal in a local home.
